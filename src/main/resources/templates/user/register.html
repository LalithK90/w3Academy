W3 Campus Terms and Conditions

Attendance

When you attend to the classes, punch the student registration card to the QR Code Reader which is placed in the front office and keep the relevant rack.

It is compulsory to maintain 80% of attendance rate for theory and practical classes.

If any student has absented for a particular class, he/she should bring a formal letter from the parent mentioning the reason for the absenteeism and hand over the letter to the class teacher.

Students should sign the in time and the out time in the attendance sheet.

It is possible to leave early from a class submitting an authorized letter from the parent or guardian.

Student should reach the class 5 minutes before the class stars.

Payment

Payment schemes are indicated in the student agreement and the student should pay the installments on or before the due date Payment can be paid by cash, cheques and credit/debit cards.

If any payment has delayed more than 7 days and it is inactivated by the system student have to pay an additional amount up to Rs. 2500/= for the delayed period. (If there is any financial difficulty to pay the amount, student should have to inform the reason to our front office two days before the due date)

If there is any additional payment it will be informed to the parent/guardian via a printed letter or email from the official email with signature of responsible person of the branch (Manager or Assistance Manager)

Courses Postpones, Branch Transfer and Batch Transfer

All the course Postpones, Branch Transfer and Batch Transfer should be informed to the Management by a formal request letter.

We are not accountable for all the non-reported Postpones, Branch Transfers and Batch Transfers. The students will be automatically suspended by the system, in such case and they have to re-register for the course
